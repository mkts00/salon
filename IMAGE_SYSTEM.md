## Rules

- Do not generate or substitute imagery.
- Do not use stock photography.
- Do not add fictional staff or customers.
- Build section dimensions around each image's composition and aspect ratio.
- Cropping, masking, overlays, color correction, and optimization are allowed
  only when they preserve the original atmosphere and authenticity.
- Keep source copies in `work/supplied-originals/`; production uses
  high-quality optimized copies of those originals.
- Do not upscale or generate replacement derivatives.
